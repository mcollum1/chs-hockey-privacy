Privacy Policy CHS Hockey Summer Training App · Last updated: May 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how the CHS Hockey Summer Training App ("App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information about users, including users under 18 years of age. By using the App you agree to the practices described below. 1. Who Can Use the App The App is a private tool for members of the Cheshire High School varsity hockey team. Access requires a coach-issued invite code. The App is not open to the general public. 2. Information We Collect We collect only what is needed to run the summer training program: Account information — name, email address, and (if Sign in with Apple is used) an Apple-anonymized email relay. Profile information — player position (forward, defense, or goalie) and graduation year / class year. Workout data — which workouts were completed and when, and optional notes or performance scores entered by the player. Device information — standard iOS session tokens used by Supabase to keep you logged in. No advertising identifiers are collected. We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, microphone input, camera input, or any health/biometric data. 3. How We Use This Information To let players log and track their workouts throughout the summer program. To let coaches view team-wide training progress and completion rates. To calculate leaderboard standings visible to all team members. To authenticate users and keep sessions secure. We do not use personal information for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to running the hockey program. 4. Information Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for their own purposes. Information is shared only as follows: Within the team — a player's first name and workout completion status are visible to coaches and, in aggregate on the leaderboard, to other players. Supabase — our database and authentication provider (supabase.com). Data is stored on Supabase servers. See Supabase's Privacy Policy. Apple — if you use Sign in with Apple, your authentication is handled by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy. Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law. 5. Children's Privacy The App is used by high school athletes, some of whom may be under 18. We take this seriously: Access is restricted to team members via a coach-controlled invite code system. We do not knowingly collect information from children for advertising, profiling, or commercial purposes. We collect only the minimum information needed to run the training program. Coaches (not players) control access and can request data deletion at any time. Parents or guardians of players under 13 who have questions or wish to have their child's data removed may contact us at the address below. 6. Data Retention and Deletion Player data is retained for the duration of the program and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. If you would like your account and associated data deleted, contact the coach or email us directly. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request. 7. Security Data is stored in a secured Supabase database with row-level security policies that ensure players can only access their own data, and coaches can access team data. Connections are encrypted in transit (TLS). We cannot guarantee absolute security but take reasonable steps to protect your information. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to coaches, who will inform the team. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 9.
